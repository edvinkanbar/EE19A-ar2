Portfolio

Previous work

I started my career at the sales floor at H&M. Their business had just bloomed, and they were the label on everyone’s lips. I got an internship where I quickly fell in love with the connection I got from the customers – and the insane amount of feedback I got for my customer service.

But a few comments I received stuck to my mind and heart. Many of them was about me being able to lift the details in every product I managed to sell to them, or the fact that I knew exactly how to market the different items by describing what they would go with, what situation to use it in and where to dress in a particular way.

The real deal

That lead me to my first real job ever.

Originally, I was hired to recruit customers for the customer clubs that the company had in the different shopping malls in Scandinavia, but they quickly learned that my ability to sell and advertise was beyond what they were offering me, so 6 months later I became the youngest employee to ever be responsible for 11 shopping malls. Over 1000 stores and also have the ability to recruit a sales team that was out working the fields I was employed for in the beginning.

A big leap? Don’t think so.

When we felt that my job was done at the startup, and it was time for me to spread my wings wider, I decided to move to jobs where I could combine the skills I’ve learned at Light Agency together with my intense curiosity to learn more.

2018-2019

Nordic Marketing Manager | Colart

With this role, I put my knowledge to use and showed off my skills by being the head of the Nordic market for 7 world leading fine-art brands. Along with responsibility for my recruited employees and strategy planning for sales training courses, I got to create events and campaigns for our various brands resellers in the Nordic market.

2017-2019 - Freelancer

Editor | Dagens Industri

I wrote articles and worked with copywriting for print & digital publications in a luxury fashion and consumption magazine. I used WordPress and Dotmailer to write my work, with the focus on marketing various brands in the luxury segment.

2016-2018

Store Manager | Victoria’s Secret

In this role, I had responsibility of staff & budgeting where the focus was on additional sales, training, and planning of physical campaigns in the store. I also had to design and create sales competitions to motivate my staff and analyze key figures hour by hour in order to improve our results on a daily basis. It also included scheduling for a larger (10+) number of employees and regularly recruiting new people at store openings or holidays.

2014-2016 - Project employment

PR Consultant | Aftonbladet AB

The role for this position meant that I was primarily responsible for the Swedish newspaper's exposure in stores, with the goal of raising sales figures and achieving daily budget goals for each area in Stockholm. The work involved daily travel by company car and careful planning of visual marketing depending on the store's design, size, customer traffic and planogram.

2013-2016 - Freelancer

Journalist | Butikstrender

Entertainment & trade writer for one of Sweden's largest trade magazines. The position included event photography, interviewing, copywriting, news journalism, and planning of both internal and public events. It was also important to stay up to date and keep up with trends in the food, trade and entertainment industry, and to always be one (or a few!) steps ahead of the competition.

2011-2013

Broadcast Journalist | WUSA9 TV

Journalist & reporter for one of the largest TV stations in the United States. WUSA9 has both a global and local journalistic focus where local news and programs are most popular. I worked mainly covering press conferences held by Barack Obama and Joe Biden, in Capitol Hill - Washington D.C. I also wrote press releases, newsletters, newspaper articles, and blog posts for the station.
